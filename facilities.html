Why Stay

At Whylaway Boarding Kennels & Cattery we really do care about your pets and we all love what we do. So next time you have to leave your pet behind for a while, leave pet care to the professionals. We specialise in care and overnight boarding allowing your pet to:

  • Stay where he/she's welcome (unlike many holiday hotels)
  • Enjoy the comfort and hygiene of hammock-style beds
  • Be monitored by onsite owner/manager of the business (family owned and operated)
  • Have access to fresh tank water with the automatic water drinkers
  • Safe and secure boarding with small dogs kept separate to medium/large dogs to avoid any intimidation
  • Enjoy exercise and playtime with safe, outdoor runs where dogs can free-run four times a day.
  • Cats enclosures are decent sizes with the option of having outside time.
  • Quality and fully balanced diet for feedtime, catering for needs of mature dogs and puppies. Special diets supplied by owners is given to pets FREE of charge.
  • Medication is given FREE of charge. Must be labelled with dosage instructions.
